About Us

Learn more about the Passport Seva Project and our mission

Our Mission

The Passport Seva Project aims to provide a simple, efficient, and transparent process for citizens to apply for and obtain a passport. We strive to make the passport application process as convenient and user-friendly as possible, ensuring that applicants receive timely and accurate services.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the leader in passport issuance services by leveraging technology and innovative practices. We aim to enhance the experience of applicants through continuous improvement and adaptation to their needs.

Who We Are

The Passport Seva Project is a flagship initiative of the Ministry of External Affairs, Government of India. We are dedicated to providing passport-related services to citizens in a seamless and efficient manner. Our team consists of committed professionals working tirelessly to ensure the highest standards of service delivery.
